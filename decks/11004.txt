Deck Name: If Bram Played !Toreador
Description:
I took Bram's last to TWDs ("nobody bounces like Schumi does" and "GP BRUSSELS -
Blame it on Didi") switched to !Toreador and added some of my own seasoning, mixed it
up, and this was the result. Big thanks to Bram for solid starting decklists.

The deck performed differently in each round and some cards were strong in one round,
but did nothing in another. Overall the bounce component was gold in every game.  I
totally picked the meta having enough bleed to make this deck shine.
