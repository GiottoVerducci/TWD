The seventh edition of Tragic Love Affair took place as a side event for the Finnish Nationals. Whereas the Nationals had a somewhat surprising political meta, one might have expected the side event to lean more towards combat. The reality was actually quite bleedy, but there was huge variation: while the fastest table finished in 38 minutes (with five players!), there were others that timed out completely. The final was one of the latter.
