Deck Name: Só Carta Justa
Description: This deck name translates from brazilian portuguese as "Only fair cards". I thought of this concept because I think that the newer anarch cards, specially those two that recquire barons (organized resistence and bait and switch) are grossly overpowered. So yeah, I'll make a deck around using those cards in the most efficient way, with the cheapest vampires possible, and see how it goes. The whole concept of a gun wall came from those two cards.

It turned out ok. It's not a fast paced deck by any means, but it's virtually unkillable. I constantly stayed in the 2-5 pool range and never got close to getting ousted. Then when you achieve stabilty you either lounge with Club Illusion + Monkey Wrench + Reckless Agitation or you grind your prey with constant revolution + army of rats + creeping sabotage + smilling jack.
