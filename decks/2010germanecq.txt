Deck Name: Govern the Gates

Description: IMHO G4/5 has an insanely good weenie to midcap crypt
for POT and DOM/POT. Inspired by Tuomas Nyberg's TWD "Dr. Sutphens
Dental Care" I used the new crypt, replaced Computer Hackings with
Govern and some cards for my personal taste. Didn't get to play the
deck before the qualifier and really only wanted to qualify, so I'm
very pleased with it's performance. Never used the whole library so
should probably go down to 80 or even 75 cards.
