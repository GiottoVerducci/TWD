Deck Name: Kiasyd Fastball

Just wind up and throw the high cheese! Gremlins and Bonding for 3
bleed at 2 stealth. Nocturns to get in the way of rushers. Sudden
Reversals to shut down Villein and other shenanigans. It probably
wants a couple more On the Qui Vive (instead of a couple Wakes) and
Delaying Tactics (instead of a couple Deflections). Foreshadowing
Destruction should probably be another Song of Pan. Scourge of the
Enochians should probably go in there, and perhaps The Uncoiling.
