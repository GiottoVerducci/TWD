Deck Name: Freaky Politicians
Created by: Jeroen van Oort

An old skool Ventrue deck focussing on putting blood on uncontrolled
vampires or use those cards to bleed heavily. Older versions had more
Freak Drives. Thats were the name came from. This deck won the 2011
Belgian National Championship on March 13, 2011 in the Outpost Gamestore
in Brussels.
