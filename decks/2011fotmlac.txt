Deck Name: Carna Takes the Subway to Work

Carna tools up with equipment and sometimes plays Govern the Unaligned
at superior. The other minions stay untapped or provide light pressure
via bleeding as the game state dictates. When your prey is vulnerable,
lunge with Misdirection, Pentex Subversion, Govern and Conditioning.
