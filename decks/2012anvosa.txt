Deck Name: The Bleeding Vignes

A very slightly tweaked version of Ruben Feldman's original "The Bleeding
Vignes" deck. Added Owain Evans for increased table talk, bargaining power
and blood management. Archon Investigation and Direct Intervention for added
defence against uncontrollable heavy bleeders and for protection directly
after lunging.
