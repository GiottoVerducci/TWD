Deck Name: Valley of the Shadows of Death

Description: A rearrangement of my Nocturn/Shadow Twin no repeat
crypt deck that always fared well. I decided to make Isanwayen
more important and explore his special. Not as no-brainer obvious
as the other 3 multi-master phase action vamps, I decided to try
and exploit him anyway since I hadn't seen any decent non-straight
up stealth bleed versions. Could use some tweaking but I only put
it together a few days before the tournament.
