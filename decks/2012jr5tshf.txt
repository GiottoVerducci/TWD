Deck Name: Mora AKA Princes Outsource Their Stealth

Description: The idea is to be able to consistently bleed for
four at three stealth and then I added the ridiculously good
defense module of Second Tradition. You need one vampire with
superior obfuscate and then preferably a different vampire
with the Prince title and presence since veiling and playing
Second Tradition and Majesty takes blood. Parity Shift because
why not - the easiest vote to negotiate through.
