Deck Name: Vita Kränkta Män ("White violated men")

Description: I made this deck for Sofia as a newbie deck
to show how votes work. The deck is a normal Govern chain
with Democritus at the top, vote if you can, otherwise
discard vote cards during referendums to draw more bleed.
