Deck Name: This Isn't That Kind of a Deck, Honest!

No Mytherceria other than the 5 Gremlins this deck relies on simply
Obtenebration and Dominate. No way of taking blood off of Vampires
since they tend to use it for playing the cards. Nocturns help against
rush and allowed for a high amount of On the Qui Vive and therefore
Redirections compared to Deflections. The name comes from me when
people expected the deck to be full of mytherceria cards.
