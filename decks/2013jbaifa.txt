Deck Name: Asylum Inmates Say "No Bueno"

Blood Dolls & Asylum HG for pool/blood management and
Pulses, Cornelius, and Ozmo for plus bleeds at stealth.

at a tournament and dubbed it "Anatole Says 'No Bueno'",
thus the title.
