Deck Name: DOM, +1 stealth, what else?

An old deck slightly tweaked after looking at Kakarot and Lönkka's TWDs.
DOM and permanent +1 Stealth is amazing, and an early Heart of Nizchetus
allows to run a huge Wash + reactions package without sacrificing the
offensive cardflow. So you can afford to take it slowly, Govern your
whole uncontrolled region out, tool up and wait for the opening.
