Deck Name: Cappadocian Powersleaze

Description: Giovanni Powerbleed with a Fortitude twist - get Lady Constancia or
Ignazio Giovanni out to bleed, Freak Drive, and remain standing for defense.
If you can get two FOR minions into play, this deck moves very fast with solid defense.
Special thanks to Adam Hulse for the late night deck clinic and Jay Kristoff for the pre-finals pep talk.
