Deck Name: Blacken the Cursed Sun!

Description: Raise Guillaume (or convert and jake) for a setup, then locations to increase hand size as usual.
Govern down until 4-5 minions in table, deflecting/delaying to control table situation. Then bleed with Kiasyds/Lasombras and Sudario for needed support.
Converts were added due to heavy Anarch Revolt metagame.
Cristobal Ghiberti very useful for Sudario, and Scourge and Anthelios discarded on most of the games.
Not an easy deck to play, but very crafty (and deadly) one!
