Deck Name: Goldfish and Clowns
Created by: Mark Loughman

Alex Romano won the Last Chance Qualifier at Origins in 2015 with this deck and
I finally got a win with it myself. It abuses Malgorzata's special ability to
get out big vampires at a discount. It doesn't seek combat, but has a robust
'hit-back' module to discourage those who do.
