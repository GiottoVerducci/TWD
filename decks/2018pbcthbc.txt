Deck Name: !Ventrue Toolbox

The classic !Ventrue grinder, but geared towards Under Siege. Fortitude to
deal with the occasional nasty combat deck, bounce for bleed or blocking,
and some permacept to wear down stealth decks. I chose to focus on using
more Governs over pool recursion in order to reliably get out more minions
and also have extra bleed. The Govern chain is optimized for multiple
Governs at DOM per turn, situation permitting.
