Deck Name: Gangrene Bleed

This brew is a remake of my first tournament deck from 2004. According to my
deck building philosophy back then, this deck tries to accrue a critical mass
of permanent effects and win in the late game. Hatch the Viper was insanely
strong and in hindsight I'd probably adjust the deck to rely more on it.
