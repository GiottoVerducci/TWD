Canberra, Australia

Craig Johnston

Deck Name: The Best of Both Worlds

Description: What can I say? It's a Malkavian/!Malkavian
Stealth Bleed deck, utilising (in my opinion) the best vampires from
each Clan in terms of bleed potential, without being too large in
it's Crypt capacity. It's not terribly harsh in it's bleed capacity,
only bleeding for 4 at most without any static bleed modifiers. It
also has quite a large capacity for blood gain (although not
excessivly so). Not as much defence as I would normally like in a
bleed deck (which tend to come under combat fire regularly) but the
Lunatics are not known for their combat evasion.
