Deck Name: Oh Darling, Peter's Nosferatu LOVE L.A.!

Rush'n'crush for offense; Rush'n'crush for defense. The name of this
deck comes from the fact that Pete Venters and L.A. Williams designed
the majority of the artwork contained herein. Oh, and I mustn't
forget the fact that this monster _is_ ('based' on) a work by
Peter 'HULK SMASH!' Bakija. May I make Peter proud!
