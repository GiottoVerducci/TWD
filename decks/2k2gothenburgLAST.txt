Deck Name: Malk antitribu slowbleeder

Description: A simple to use bleed deck, usually bleeds for 2 or 3 and
bloats with Kindred spirits and Blood of the sabbat. The key to
winning is Secure haven. With only one active protected vampire, the
combat decks have to fight someone else.
