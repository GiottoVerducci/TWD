Deck Name: 15 Minutes of Fame

Dominate/fortitude/protean with the dual capability of bruise 'n bleed
and rush/fame. Defensive mechanisms are deflection and upstream
rush. Lazverinus is the 'star player,' but isn't essential to the
deck. Intercept is forgone in lieu of more focused rush and some
political activity.
