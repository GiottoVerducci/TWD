Deck Name: Masking the Unblockable

Description: Combining Aching Beauty and Toreador Grand Ball may look
like a strange strategy. Add in Mask of a thousand Faces and it makes
more sense.
Declare non-bleed actions with your unblockable vampire. Play Mask and
finish the action in order to get another unblockable action.
Try playing bleed-modifiers and Aching Beauty/Camarilla Exemplary on
different vampires. Declare bleeds for lots. If blocked, Mask in some
pool-punishment. If bounced, Mask in a lower amount of bleed.
Expensive mastercards go hand in hand with Parity Shift.

Doing this deck again I'd throw out all equipment and add another three
Majesty.
