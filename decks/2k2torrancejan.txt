This is the exact deck I used during the tourny, there were some
changes from the normal version as I'm experimenting with it some.
The beauty of this deck is you can bleed anyone(gaining pool) and
reverse alot, thus even with little or no votes(5 max) or combat you
can still have a say in whats going on. In this Tournament, the
second qualifing round and the final round I back bled almost the
whole time.
