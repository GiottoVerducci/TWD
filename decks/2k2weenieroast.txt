Seekonk Weenie Roast
Seekonk, Massachusetts
June 29th 2002
24 players
Matthew Hirsch

Deck Name: "The Gun is My Brush, Your Face is My Canvas"

Description: Bloat up and build a horde of low-caps
plus Anneke or Anson. Give guns to the ones with celerity.
Swarm and destroy.

The offense isn't as fast as other tourney decks, but
once it gets rolling it has pretty strong momentum.
Small caps with guns and superior Auspex can run a
tight defense to allow time for that longer build-up.
