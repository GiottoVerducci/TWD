Orgy of Flesh - Breathe Harder Remix
Description: This deck is a modification to the Orgy of Flesh Palla
Grande/Vic deck. This adds the combat module used in Bow Down Before
Matteus, specifically the Breath of the Dragon and Rotschreck. Ideally
you focus on creating Creation Rites vampires with vic, and then they
either bleed when the Palla Grande is out, or Art Scam when it's not.
Non vic vampires do maintenence actions or get bleed defense duty.
Newly created Creation Rites can use Breath/Rotschreck to deter pesky
actions against you. Always influence out a titled vampire first, as
without the additional minions this deck might stall. With a titled
vampire out, the deck starts to roll almost immediately if the
Creation Rites are not blocked.
