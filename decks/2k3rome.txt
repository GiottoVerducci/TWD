Camarilla Threat
Rome, Italy
March 2nd 2003
14 players
Marco La Valle

"Mr. Majestic"

A horde of small cap vampires with PRE relentlessly bleed and vote,
taking multiple actions in the same turn. Charming lobby, awe and
bewitching orations provide huge amount of votes, and voter
captivations replenish your vampires, so allowing unlimited use of
superior Majesty. Expect everybody at the table to turn against you:
which is good, since you will cycle even faster through your library!
This deck will be completely shut down by Atonement, so use your
Hostile Takeover judiciously (a second one will be necessary, when
this list will be made public).
