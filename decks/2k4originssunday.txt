Deck Name: The Blockinator

Description: Group 1-2 AUS-CEL Toreador Princes with Concealed
.44's and a big pile of intercept, untap, and Celerity cards.
All the Princes (save for Volker) have +1 bleed, so there's
built-in ousting power once you've gotten your prey's vampires
smushed. The Rack, Art Museum, Hunting Ground, and a wad of
Blood Dolls provide a solid pool-gaining infrastructure.
