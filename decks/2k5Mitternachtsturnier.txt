Deck Name: Amazing Belly Necklace

Straightforward Ventrue Law Firm. 16 Majesty instead of Freak Drive
and Fortitude Prevent Damage cards for better cardflow and better
combat defense against CEL (Idea shamelessly stolen from Josh Duffin).
Last minute changes were the addition of Carlton Van Wyk (killer!) and
D.I (saved my ass once more). Never underestimate the value of
Powerbase: Rome. esp. with Anson where you can add 2 counters per turn.
Tournament last until Sunday 16th. It was over at 3am ;-)
