Deck Name: säävarauksella

Deck Description: The idea of the deck is to use the Escaped Mental
Patients with Flash Grenades for tapping, Revelations and Change of
Targets to get rid of Wakes and permanent bleed enhancers with some
Dementation support for ousting. The EMPs are also great at slowing
down too aggressive predators (or any players, for that matter) and
defending against rushes and such.
