Deck Name: Bugs Bunny

The trick of this deck is the Bugs Bunny maneuver; do you remember when
Bugs go through the earth to kick the ass of Sam or Daffy duck? This
deck does the same; bleed, bleed, and more bleed, and if someone tries
to stop you or comes to hit you; you'll have the Protean to protect
yourself. Use "Flesh of Marble" to defeat Immortal Grapple, or the
simple and excellent Bugs's maneuver: dig and use "Earth Meld".
