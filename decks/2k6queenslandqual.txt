Deck Name: Bouncin' Beast

A Beast potence rush deck with a supporting cast of smaller
potence/dominate vamps. It has some bounce capability so you don't
have to spend your whole life going backwards if your predator is
stealth bleed (quite likely!). Swallowed by the Night lets you
counter enemy maneuver and stealth past chump blockers to hit key vamps.
