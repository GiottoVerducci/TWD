Deck Name: Guedado Where Are You?

Description: This is the deck I won the tournament with. The "correct"
version, (was missing the burn cards right before I left for the
tournament) is a 61 card deck,removing a Parthenon, adding Specialization
and Brothers Grimm, and turning all the burn cards into the same master card
burn card. I love the fact the Guadado is the "key" vampire, being both Tzimisce
and Laibon (and has obf). Ana Rita Montana and a Laibon will do in a pinch.
The Charisma/PB: Tshawane is great for allies, as well as reducing costs
for cards like Memories.
