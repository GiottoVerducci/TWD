DECK NAME: "Call of Kooks"
I did indeed end up winning. Although I like to think of my deck as
less of a bleed deck and more of a toolboxy Minion factory, since I
use (4) Grooming the Protege and (15) The Call. My bleed package is
actually quite low with (3) Madman's Quill, (2) Confusion, and (4)
Eyes of Chaos. There is a decent amount of stealth, but not obscene
with (17) stealth cards (not including the Confusions and Random
Patterns in superior). The deck performed well all day, but there are
definately things I would tweak. I would remove a few (probably 3) of
The Call and add a few more bleed cards and/or votes.
