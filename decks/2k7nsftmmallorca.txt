Deck Name: Mad Dancers

Description: Bring out the highest capacity vampire in your uncontrolled
region. Once he or she is in play, play Danse Macabre on him/her and have
him/her perform two actions per turn (The Call & bleed, most of the time,
or Revelations & bleed, etc.). Thus, you should be able to put a few
vampires in play at a low cost and quickly. The rest is easy: bleed your
preys to death:)
