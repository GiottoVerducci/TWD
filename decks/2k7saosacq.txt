SAC Qualifier
São Paulo, Brazil
June 17th 2007
40 players
David Magri da Silva

Deck Name: Baratas'07 (In english: Roaches'07)

Description: Uses embraces to create a horde of weenies, gains tons of
pool with ConBoon + Foundation Exhibit and massive bleed with Palla
Grande. The deck doesn't use disciplines, so he can use many
discipline hoser Gehenna cards.

This is a very effective deck, which already won many other
tournaments in Brazil. In fact, David has gained the alias The Roach
King, for it. In this tournament, he ended the final table with more
than 40 pool and something around 16 minions, but I remember another
one where he ended with more than 120 pool and more than 20 minions. A
deck to be feared.
