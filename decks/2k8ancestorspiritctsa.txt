Deck Name: Klaus but no cigar

Like most reactionary builds this deck is fairly slow moving. The idea
is to get the tag-team combo of Venere and Klaus out, block key
actions, grab a .44 and hole up until the table plays out. Once other
decks start losing their legs begin turning the screws with a few big
bleeds (Palla Grande/Aire of Elation), playing a Smiling Jack and
dropping a strategic Pentex Subversion. The lower cap vamps are there
purely for weenie support; playing the odd Art Scam and for the Palla
Grande bleed push. As always, I over did the master card count. ;)
This deck won with 4VP in the final table and 6.5VP in the Prelim
rounds.
