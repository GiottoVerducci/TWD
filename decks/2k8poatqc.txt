Deck Name: Foundation for the Win

Description: It's pretty straightforward. You kine, you con boon and
you bleed until you win. You'd better be good at looking weak because
it's hard to say you're not a threat when you got 7 minions bleeding
for two and having a vote. The deck is based on a foundation deck I
found on the web, retooled without the gehenna cards to get less table
hate.
