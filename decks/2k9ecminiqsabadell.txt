Note: most people ask why do I play Tupdog here, there are plenty of
reasons indeed: permits me not to repeat vampires which grants better
crypt cycling, once on table they can recruit a generic ally (w/o fear
of being blocked and sent to torpor) before vanishing, they give you
specific rush defense, may play delaying tactics, and in the ash-heap
they may turn into wraiths once KD is full... though it' s a pity
they are non-unique.

After the Tournament I would only add a Vox Domini, maybe instead of
Giant' s Blood
