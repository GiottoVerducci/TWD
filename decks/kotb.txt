Deck Name: Kiss of the Brujah

Description: Fairly standard Brujah princes deck. Bleed with every vamp every round, if
they block put them in torpor. Use the 2nds to wake and block your prey's
vamps from hunting, voting etc. and your predator from bleeding, voting etc.
A few rush actions to take out any annoying vamps like Leandro, Arika etc.
